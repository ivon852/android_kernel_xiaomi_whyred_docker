QTI Virtual Clock Frontend Binding
------------------------------------------------

Required properties :
- compatible : shall contain:
		"qcom,virtclk-frontend-8996"

- #clock-cells : shall contain 1
- #reset-cells : shall contain 1

Example:
	virtclk-frontend@0 {
		compatible = "qcom,virtclk-frontend-8996";
		#clock-cells = <1>;
		#reset-cells = <1>;
	};
